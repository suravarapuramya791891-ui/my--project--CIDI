Water Supply

GVMC provides reliable water supply services including installation, billing, and maintenance.

  • New water connection application
    Apply online for new water connections easily with required documents.
  • Water bill payment online
    Pay your water bills conveniently through our online portal without waiting in queues.
  • Water leakage complaints
    Report any water leakage promptly and track the complaint status until resolution.
  • Water quality monitoring
    Regular water quality monitoring ensures safe drinking water in all areas.
